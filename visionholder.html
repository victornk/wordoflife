Word of Life Ministriesr

Bishop Stuart Shava-Chimbwanda is an Apostolic voice to the nations. He got born again at the age of 19. He entered into the ministry at the tender age of 21 in Southern Zimbabwe where he got commissioned to move to Durban in South Africa where he pastored churches in the Phoenix and Umlazi townships of Ethekwini Region.

Meet the Bishop:

Later he moved to Siyabuswa Mpumalanga where he founded WOLM. Bishop Shava is a well sought out minister of the Word of God in Zimbabwe and around Mpumalanga in different Christian gathering. He has been serving as an Apostolic Father and mentor to ministries. He and his wife Mam Sbongile Shava, serve as the senior pastors of Word of Life Christian Ministries situated in Siyabuswa Maganagobuswa with 3 branches (Marble Hall, Kwamhlanga and Ga-Matla) and blessed with 3 children
